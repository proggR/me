Profile

I am a web developer living in Kingston, Ontario. I work for Queen's University on an open source learning platform called Entrada. I am also the founder and developer of sourced.fm, a social crowdfunding and sales platform built to connect musicians to their fans and each other in new ways. I have a passion for development, music, business, and solving problems with intuitive, efficient, extensible, and scalable solutions.
